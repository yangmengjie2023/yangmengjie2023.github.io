Research Focus

I explore emergent phenomena in non-Hermitian topology, quantum many-body systems, and quantum circuits. My work centers on non-Hermitian skin effects, exceptional spectral structures, and critical dynamics at the interface of topology, dissipation, and quantum coherence.
