About

I study open quantum and wave systems where gain, loss, dissipation, and non-reciprocal dynamics reshape spectra, localization, topology, and collective behavior.

I am currently looking for postdoctoral opportunities starting in 2027, with broad interests in condensed matter theory, quantum many-body physics, open quantum systems, topology, and nonequilibrium dynamics.

Research Interests

  • Open and non-Hermitian quantum systems
  • Topological phases and boundary phenomena
  • Quantum many-body dynamics
  • Nonequilibrium and dissipative physics
